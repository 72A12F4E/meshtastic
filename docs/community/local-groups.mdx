Listed below are some groups that have organized to build out Meshtastic networks in their area. Reach out to them
for help getting started or if you would like to contribute to the network. If you are a group organizer and have a
web presence, please add your group to the list by editing this page, or contacting us on
[discord](https://discord.com/invite/ktMAKGBnBs).
